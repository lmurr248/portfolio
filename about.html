// About Me

I've been building websites for over 12 years, initially focusing on front-end development using HTML and CSS. In recent years, I've expanded my skill set to encompass both client-side and server-side JavaScript, and I've also dabbled in Python, Ruby, C#, and PHP.

My original path in life was in architectural design, earning a 2:1 degree in Architecture in 2009. Over time, my passion for designing built structures transitioned into digital web design and development, where my primary interest and passion now lies.
